Résumé

— I'm a haute couture model. Creativity is my passion. I’m really interested in theatre. I’m pretty open to a lot of things, which makes me enjoy fashion and making new experiences within it. My approach to life has proved favourable amongst clients in the fashion world. Brands like Maison Margiela and Louis Vuitton constantly include me in projects. I'm represented by Viva Model Management.

Experience

  • 2015

    Began studying Theatre Theory and Politics at the Freie Universität in Berlin.

    The same year was scouted while waiting for a bus, and signed the contract with the German agency Tomorrow Is Another Day. The next 5 years allow building up my modelling proficiency and become reputable within the world of fashion.

    Also in 2015, participated in the first runway show for Sacai.

  • 2019

    Came one of the biggest moments. I was personally chosen by John Galliano to close out the Maison Margiela show. After the show became a prominent figure and consistent face in the industry.

    Became Breakout Star by The Models.com - Model of the Year Industry Awards as a newcomer to the fashion industry.

  • 2020

    Became one of the Top 50 male models.
